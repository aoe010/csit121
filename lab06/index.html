Review

"Equalizer" is a gripping action thriller that showcases Denzel Washington's commanding performance as Robert McCall, a man with a mysterious past who seeks to right the wrongs of society. The film's intense action sequences and thought-provoking themes make it a must-watch for fans of the genre.
